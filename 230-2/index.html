The Brief

The 'Laid Back Bike' project builds on the School of Engineering's Aim93 initiative which aims to break the speed record for a human power vehicle.

Working in teams, first year Product Design & Engineering Product Design students have designed and prototyped recumbent cycles using old bikes, bamboo, fibre glass tape, epoxy resin, blood, sweat and tears as key structural elements.
