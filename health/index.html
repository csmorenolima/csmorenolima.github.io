App Prototype

The Brief

Develop an engineered solution from concept through to artefact. The artefact may be physical and/or digital. Alongside this, present a detailed record of the design development process, and an analytical report that demonstrates the application of human centred design expertise to the proposed solution.

The project is an opportunity to showcase engineering problem-solving skills in the health and education sectors, focusing on innovative solutions that address real-world challenges.

The Story

This initiative proposes a combination of physical products and a dedicated application to be introduced across UK educational institutions. Its goal is to empower and support young people in education by tackling period poverty and the stigma that surrounds it.
