The Story

A nail-biting fantasy adventure, in a world full of dangerous creatures. Once, the land and sea flourished under the rule of mighty kings and queens, but darkness descended upon the kingdoms. Amidst the chaos, great kingdoms crumbled and people scattered across the plains.


From the far reaches of the realm, disparate factions converged to reclaim their land and dignity. United by shared loss, they forged alliances born of necessity. Yet within the ranks of these allies, treachery and strife lurked - old grievances resurfaced and the quest for redemption grew tainted by ambition and betrayal.
