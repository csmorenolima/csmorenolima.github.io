The Brief

Design a digital tool that harnesses and increases the quantity and quality of citizens’ participation in democratic processes. Investigate the barriers to citizen participation in voting and decision making, and identify a target group and explore their needs.

The Proposal

BrightUP is an educational digital tool for schools that utilises the increase use of technology and gamification among young people to teach and expand their knowledge on politics. To include the app in the teaching curriculum means giving the younger generation a voice in democratic processes and encourage citizen participation.
