OVERVIEW

The SCAAR project focuses on advanced transportation design and recumbent cycle development.

THE BRIEF

Working collaboratively, first-year Product Design and Engineering Product Design students designed and prototyped recumbent cycles using reclaimed bicycles, bamboo, fiberglass tape, and epoxy resin as primary structural materials. The project emphasised teamwork, sustainable practices and hands-on engineering experience.

The Story

The 'Laid Back Bike' project builds on the School of Engineering's Aim93 initiative which aims to break the speed record for a human power vehicle.
